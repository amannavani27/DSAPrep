# Tech Interview Prep

A mobile app to help you prepare for technical interviews with flashcard-style learning for Data Structures & Algorithms and System Design topics.

## Features

- **DSA Topics**: Arrays, Trees, Graphs, Dynamic Programming, and more
- **System Design Topics**: Scalability, databases, caching, microservices, and real-world architectures
- **Swipe-based Learning**: Swipe left if you know it, right to review later
- **Progress Tracking**: Track your mastery across all topics
- **Cloud Sync**: Sign in to sync your progress across devices

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Is the app free?**
Yes, Tech Interview Prep is completely free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

**Do I need an account?**
No, you can use the app without an account. However, creating a free account allows you to sync your progress across devices.

**How do I reset my progress?**
You can reset your progress from the Profile tab in the app.
